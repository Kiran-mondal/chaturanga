📜 Pieces & Rules Dictionary

  • Raja (King) ⚜️: Moves exactly 1 square in any direction (orthogonal or diagonal).
  • Mantri (Minister) 📜: Moves exactly 1 square diagonally only (precursor to the Queen).
  • Gaja (Elephant) 🐘: Moves exactly 2 squares diagonally, jumping over intervening pieces.
  • Ashva (Horse) 🐎: Moves in standard 'L' shape (2 steps straight and 1 to the side), jumping allowed.
  • Ratha (Chariot) 🛕: Moves orthogonally (straight lines along ranks/files) any number of vacant cells.
  • Padati (Infantry) ⚔️: Moves 1 step straight forward, captures 1 step diagonally forward.
